Who We Are

Built on compassion.
Rooted in Ghana.

The Salih Cares Foundation is a Ghanaian non-profit organization focused on community welfare and mental health support. We go where the need is greatest — to underserved communities across Ghana — with direct, dignified interventions.

From food relief in times of crisis, to mental health awareness programmes in schools and communities, to supporting widows and orphans — we act where action is needed most.

Our Founder

A vision born from
genuine compassion.

Mrs. Tenny Salih Basintale founded the Salih Cares Foundation with a singular belief — that true change begins at the community level, delivered with dignity and heart.

Drawing from her own experiences witnessing mental health struggles and poverty go unaddressed in Ghanaian communities, she built a foundation that bridges the gap between need and care — providing direct, meaningful interventions where they are needed most.

Under her leadership, Salih Cares has grown from a small local effort into a recognised force for good across multiple regions of Ghana, touching thousands of lives and breaking the stigma around mental health one community at a time.
