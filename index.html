Who We Are

Rooted in Ghana,
growing for Africa

Nnipa Ne Asase (meaning "People and the Earth" in Twi) is a Ghanaian-led environmental trust dedicated to reversing deforestation, restoring watersheds, and empowering local communities as guardians of their land.

We work hand-in-hand with farmers, chiefs, youth groups, and District Assemblies across Ghana — building solutions that respect our culture and our land.

Why Ghana Needs This

Ghana has lost over 90% of its original forest cover.

Once blanketed in rich tropical forest, Ghana's tree cover has been devastated by illegal logging, galamsey, and unsustainable farming. The Volta Lake is shrinking. Rivers run orange with sediment. This work cannot wait.
